About Golden Movie Awards

The Golden Movie Awards is an annual event that celebrates excellence in the film industry. Established in 2017, the awards recognize outstanding achievements in acting, directing, cinematography, and more. Our mission is to honor the hard work and creativity of filmmakers and artists who bring stories to life on the big screen.

Venue

The 9th edition of the Golden Movie Awards will be held at the iconic Melbourne Cricket Stadium, Auatralia, the stadium is the perfect venue to host this prestigious event.

Date

Mark your calendars! The event will take place on May 02, 2025. Join us for an unforgettable evening of glitz, glamour, and celebration.

Voting Opens

Voting for your favorite nominees starts on April 05, 2025. Don't miss the chance to support the stars you love! Click here to vote.

Tickets Open

Tickets for the event will be available starting April 20, 2025. Secure your spot at the most anticipated event of the year! Book your tickets now.

Hosts

This year's event will be hosted by Mirchi Siva and Bavana, Sathish and Ramya. Get ready for an evening filled with laughter, entertainment, and unforgettable moments.

Timings

The event will begin at 5:00 PM and is expected to run until 12:00 PM. Make sure to arrive early to enjoy the red carpet festivities!

About

Golden Movie Awards is an annual awards ceremony honoring outstanding achievements in the film industry. The awards are presented by KVVSH group of companies to recognize excellence of professionals in the film industry.
